Is the gym open?



Yes, the gym is open. Currently only 12 people are allowed at a time and you must book a time slot with the Fitness center.

How's the quarantine procedure like?

Getting your symptom checker is very important because it allows you access different areas of the campus.You will need a Safe to report on Campus result to Public safety and a PCR test.

How many people can gather at one place?

A maximum of 6 people can be hosted in dorms and private areas. If it seen that there are more than 6 people you will be requested to leave the room and can face a fine.

Can we go off campus?


Yes you can go off campus but make sure you have a valid PCR test so that you can be cleared for when you come back. Also make sure that you have a mask with you at all times.

How is the housing like?


There is a low density model being applied on campus right now which allows people to have their own rooms with less contact with others.

Is there in person classes?



Currently there are no in-person classes. All classes are on Zoom as of now.
